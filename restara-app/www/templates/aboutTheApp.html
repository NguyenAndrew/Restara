Inspiration

Several. My mom, and how she strives to achieve the best customer experience. Also, the unnecessary "May I have the bill?" wait times at restaurants that I have been to.

What does the app do

Let's customers pay for their restaurant bill using their phone, which improves customer experience and increases the money obtained from tips

How I built the app

I prototyped the app using current web languages and Ionic.

Built with

Ionic, Angular2, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Challenges I ran into

Determining what functionality would be needed to demonstrate a working concept. Cutting out the features, so I can complete the prototype in a 12hr time limit.

Accomplishments that I am proud of

Doing research on the market impact of my app. Completing the app on time.

Whats next for Restara?

Rewritring the the app to be fully functional. Doing user experience tests at local restaurants.
